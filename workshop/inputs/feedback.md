# Canteen Feedback

We collected this informally over the last few weeks. Some students complained directly, some suggested fixes, some just commented to friends. Read the lot — patterns will emerge.

## What students said

- "I waited 25 minutes in line and they ran out of biryani right when it was my turn."
- "The line is unpredictable. Sometimes 5 minutes, sometimes 30. I never know when to leave class."
- "I wish I could lock in my food before I leave the lab — like a 'pick up at 12:30' option."
- "I'm vegetarian. I have to ask every single time which curries are veg. There's no labelling."
- "My friend is on a budget and never knows how much she's spent on canteen food this month."
- "I'd pay extra to skip the lunch rush."
- "The cashier said 'come back in 20' — I would've ordered earlier if I knew the wait time."
- "Half the time I get there, the special I wanted is sold out. There's no way to check."
- "I want to bring lunch back for two friends but I can't remember exactly what they like. Last time was guesswork."
- "I don't want my food sitting out for 30 minutes after they make it."
- "Pickup is faster but only some places do it."
- "There's no way to pay before getting there. I always have to remember cash."
- "I'm allergic to peanuts. I have no idea which dishes have peanuts in them."
- "Group orders are a nightmare — five of us trying to remember everyone's drinks."
- "When I'm running late, I'd love a 'just the same as last time' button."
- "There's no notification when my food is ready. I just have to keep checking."

## A couple of users went further and proposed fixes

- "Add pickup time slots like the bigger food apps — let me pick a 15-minute window when I order so my food is fresh."
- "A 'pick up at 12:30' option would be a game changer."
- "Show me what's left of the daily specials in real time — like a stock counter."
- "Tag every dish with veg / halal / contains-nuts. It can't be that hard."
